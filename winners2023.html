THE GAME WINNERS

Baldur's Gate 3

Baldur's Gate 3 is a critically acclaimed role-playing
game set in the rich universe of Dungeons and Dragons.
It was released fully in August 2023. Gaming transports
players on an epic journey through the Forgotten Realms,
starting with a mind flayer parasite threatening to turn
them and their friends into monsters. The game is driven
by player choice, deep characters, and a rich, branching
story where decisions have significant effects. Lauded for
its deep turn-based combat, vast world to explore, and
unprecedented player liberty, Baldur's Gate 3 was met
with universal acclaim, eventually taking home
Game of the Year at The Game Awards 2023.
