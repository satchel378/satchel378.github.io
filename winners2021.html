THE GAME WINNERS

it takes two


In 2021, published by Hazelight Studios, cooperative platform
adventure game It Takes Two is designed for two players only. It follows
Cody and May, a couple who are contemplating divorce and are
transformed magically into dolls by the tears of their daughter.
To restore them to human form, however, they have to travel
through utterly fantastical and changing worlds, co-operating
with each other's special and changing powers in each level to
defeat barriers and restore their relationship. It Takes Two's
innovative co-op gameplay and emotional narrative resonated with many,
earning it Game of the Year at The Game Awards 2021. The game
explores connection, compromise, and love through its innovative
challenges and engaging narrative.
