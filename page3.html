The Game Winners

THE LAST OF US - 2020

The Last of Us Part II is an action-adventure video game created by Naughty Dog and released only for the PlayStation 4 in June 2020. It is the sequel to The Last of Us (2013) and continues the story set in a post-apocalyptic United States that has been ruined by a mutated Cordyceps fungus that infects human beings and turns them violent infected creatures. The game centers primarily on Ellie, now an adult, on a bloody quest of revenge and self-discovery after a horribly violent and intimate tragedy. Praised for its emotionally resonant narrative, well-developed characters, and mature thematic discussions of such things as trauma, loss, and moral ambiguity, the game pushed the boundaries of interactive storytelling. The Last of Us Part II received widespread critical acclaim and took home The Game Awards 2020 Game of the Year, in addition to many other accolades including Best Narrative, Best Direction, and Best Audio Design.
