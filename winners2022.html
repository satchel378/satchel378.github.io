THE GAME WINNERS

ELDEN RING

Released in February 2022 by FromSoftware, Elden Ring is a huge
open-world action role-playing game set in the fantasy Lands Between,
a realm once blessed by the powerful Elden Ring and ruled by Queen
Marika the Eternal. Players take on the role of a Tarnished, an exiled
warrior who is called back to this shattered world once the Elden
Ring is broken and its shards, the Great Runes, are seized by Marika's
demigod children, plunging the world into chaos. The Tarnished will
embark on a dangerous journey to retrieve the Great Runes, battle
the demigods, and become the Elden Lord. It's famous for its challenging
combat, intricate lore, vast exploration, and player freedom. Elden Ring was
universally critically acclaimed and went on to win Game of the Year
at The Game Awards 2022.
